How do I enter grades and comments by student in Gradebook2?

In addition to entering grades in the spreadsheet columns, instructors can view, grade and enter comments by student.

Go to Gradebook2.

Select Gradebook2 from the Tool Menu in your site.

In the spreadsheet on the right, click on the name of the student.

This displays the Student Summary for that student.

Enter grades in grade boxes (or view student's grades).

Click Close (or Next/Previous) to view/grade next student.

To view or comment on a grade item for the student, click the Comments tab.

This displays the comments for each grade item.

Enter Comments, the click Close (or Next/Previous).
