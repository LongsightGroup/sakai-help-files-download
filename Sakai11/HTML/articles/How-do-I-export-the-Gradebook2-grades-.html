How do I export the Gradebook2 grades?

Go to Gradebook2.

Select Gradebook2 from the Tool Menu in your site.

Click Tools > Export.

This displays the Gradebook2 Export dialog box.

Select the export format and file type, then click Export.

Depending on your computer settings, you may see another download dialog box.

Download the exported Gradebook file to your computer.
